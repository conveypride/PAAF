About Us

Healthcare, Education and Entrepreneurship

Empowering African lives through innovation, education, and healthcare, we bridge communities and opportunities, ensuring a brighter future for Africa and its diaspora.

Jerry Ouner,PhD, Founder

Push Aid Africa (PAAF), Inc. is a nonprofit organization registered in the USA as 501 (c)(3) and in Ghana as a non-governmental organization. PAAF is committed to improving the lives of Africans both at home and in the diaspora through healthcare, education and entrepreneurship. We partner with academic, private, and public institutions to design innovative programs.
