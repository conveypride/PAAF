Mission & Vision

Our Mission & Vision Statements

Our Mission

To inspire hope and contribute to the advancement of healthcare and education of populations of African descent nationally and internationally through volunteerism, advocacy and philanthropy.

Our Vision

Support development of Africa as a place where everyone attains the right to quality healthcare and education.

PAAF is committed to improving the lives of Africans both at home and in the diaspora through healthcare, education and entrepreneurship.
